Welcome to My Personal Page

Posted on Wed 15 December 2021 in misc

I am a graduate student in the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering at Bilkent University and a graduate researcher at Imaging and Computational Neuroscience (ICON) Lab under the supervision of Prof. Tolga Çukur. The focal point of my research is the application of novel deep learning techniques on MRI data for image synthesis and reconstruction. I develop novel training strategies for deep learning models for accelerated MRI to improve image quality while cutting acquisition time down. In my research, I have worked on transfer learning, semi-supervised, self-supervised, unsupervised, and progressive training approaches to build deep learning models.
