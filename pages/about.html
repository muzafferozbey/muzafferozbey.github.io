About Me

I am a graduate student in the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering at Bilkent University and a graduate researcher at Imaging and Computational Neuroscience (ICON) Lab under the supervision of Prof. Tolga Çukur. The focal point of my research is the application of novel deep learning techniques on MRI data for image synthesis and reconstruction.
